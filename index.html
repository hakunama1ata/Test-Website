About GearUp

GearUp is a grassroots initiative to provide essential vehicles like pickup trucks, mopeds, and bicycles to tradesmen, technicians, and aspiring entrepreneurs. This project empowers people by removing logistical barriers and helping them take control of their work lives.

Our Mission

To empower the working population with reliable transportation solutions. These vehicles help people reach job sites, deliver services, and build independence. We don't just donate vehicles—we deliver opportunity.

Our Vision

We envision a future where access to transportation is no longer a barrier to success. We foster self-reliance, dignity, and sustainable local development by equipping motivated individuals with the tools to thrive.
